A collaborative team project to build a website that showcases our club, its vision, activities, and community.

## 📌 Project Overview
This website is designed to introduce our club to a wider audience, highlight our initiatives, and provide an easy way for people to connect with us.

## 📅 Events
This section highlights past, ongoing, and upcoming events organized by the club, keeping members and visitors informed and engaged.

## 🤖 About AI
An overview of Artificial Intelligence and its relevance to our club, including our focus areas, interests, and goals related to AI.

## 🌐 GD Section
A dedicated section for group discussions, activities, and collaborative learning initiatives conducted by the club.

## 👥 Team Members Hierarchy
This section presents the organizational structure of the club, including leadership roles and team hierarchies.

## 💻 Developers
Details of the developers who contributed to building and maintaining this website.

## 📞 Get in Touch
Contact information and links to connect with the club through email, social media, or other platforms.

## 🚀 Tech Stack
- **React** – Frontend library for building user interfaces

- **Tailwind CSS** – Utility-first CSS framework for rapid UI development
